Volunteer For NatureWorksBest

Volunteer help needed

Volunteer help is needed for statistical review and audit.  To be part of a team of unpaid volunteers gathering and analyzing data on an ever-increasing number of cancer survivors, no two with exactly the same history and circumstances (especially because we are an outpatient clinic that emphasizes patients’ medical freedom, rather than an inpatient institution coercing everybody to have the same circumstances).  Trying to make sensible comparisons and statistical inferences among very different individuals consumes enormous amounts of time and effort, and is never-ending, but may lead to interesting insights.  This work is unpaid, in order to try to prevent bias, with a team equally drawn from both sides of the medical / ideological divide.  In order to protect patient privacy and to maintain consistent analytical tools, we ask for a minimum one-year commitment.  Call us if interested in volunteering.
